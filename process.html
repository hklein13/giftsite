The Gift Clarification Process

How Discovery Unfolds

This isn't a personality test or a skills assessment. It's a process of reflection, dialogue, and mutual recognition. The most meaningful insights emerge from conversations with people who see you clearly.

Scroll to explore
01

Reflection

Begin with four core questions that help you look inward. Consider the moments when you've felt most alive, most useful, most yourself. What were you doing? Who were you with?

02

Witnessing

Share your reflections with people who know you well. Listen as they affirm and refine what you've sensed about yourself. This is where the real discovery happens — hearing from others.

03

Integration

Look for the themes that emerge from your reflections and the feedback from others. What patterns appear? What words keep surfacing? What truths resonate most deeply?

04

The Gift Statement

Name your Gift. Not as a label or a limitation, but as a compass — something you can return to when making decisions, building relationships, or finding your path forward.
