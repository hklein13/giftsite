Gift Companion

An AI-assisted companion to help you prepare for the Gift Process. It supports your reflection and helps clarify your thinking before the conversations that matter most.

How It Works

The Gift Companion is designed to guide you through thoughtful reflection before you engage in dialogue with family members or team members.

  • Guided questions to spark self-reflection
  • Helps you articulate what you sense about your Gift
  • Prepares you for meaningful conversations
  • Works at your own pace

Remember: The companion is just a tool — the real work happens between people, face to face.
