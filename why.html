Why We Exist

Helping People Discover Their Unique Gift — And the Gift in One Another

Since 1982, the Gift Process has helped more than 100,000 people uncover a deeper understanding of who they are and how they contribute to the world. What began as a simple reflective inquiry has grown into a powerful framework for personal purpose, relational insight, and the cultivation of human worth.

The Gathering Gifts Foundation was created to bring this work to people of all ages, backgrounds, and circumstances — without financial barriers.

At the heart of our mission is a simple belief:

Every person has a unique Gift.

Every Gift has value.

And the world becomes better when we recognize and appreciate one another's Gifts.

Our work combines decades of lived experience with emerging technologies — including AI — to make self-discovery more accessible, more human, and more connected than ever before.

Whether you are discovering your own Gift or witnessing someone else's, you are participating in something meaningful: the honoring of human essence.
