Why We Exist

Naming What Has Always Been True

Every person carries within them a unique Gift ‚Äî an innate way of contributing to the world that brings life to others and fulfillment to themselves.

Yet many of us move through life without ever naming this Gift. We sense it in moments of flow, in the gratitude of others, in the quiet satisfaction of work that feels meaningful. But we haven't found the words.

The Gift Site exists to help you ‚Äî and the people you care about ‚Äî discover and articulate what has always been true about you.

The Gift Clarification Process

How Discovery Unfolds

This isn't a personality test or a skills assessment. It's a process of reflection, dialogue, and mutual recognition. The most meaningful insights emerge not from algorithms, but from conversations with people who see you clearly.

01

Reflect

Begin by looking inward. Consider the moments when you've felt most alive, most useful, most yourself. What were you doing? Who were you with? What felt effortless?

02

Dialogue

Share your reflections with people who know you well. Listen as they affirm and refine what you've sensed about yourself. This is where the real discovery happens ‚Äî face to face.

03

Clarify

Name your Gift. Not as a label or a limitation, but as a compass ‚Äî something you can return to when making decisions, building relationships, or finding your path forward.

"The real power of this work lives inside of dialogue ‚Äî hearing affirming reflections from others, face to face or over the phone."

For Teams

For teams, the Gift Process supports reflection and shared understanding before conversations take place. It helps team members:

  • Clarify how they naturally contribute
  • Recognize the Gifts they bring to group efforts
  • Prepare for more meaningful, respectful dialogue
  • Gain insights about the Gifts found within fellow team members

The process helps each member enter conversations with greater clarity and intentionality, setting the stage for mutual respect and appreciation.
