Why We Exist

Every person carries within them a unique Gift — an innate way of contributing to the world that brings life to others and fulfillment to themselves.

Yet many of us move through life without ever naming this Gift. We sense it in moments of flow, in the gratitude of others, in the quiet satisfaction of work that feels meaningful. But we haven't found the words.

The Gift Site exists to help you — and the people you care about — discover and articulate what has always been true about you.

The Gathering Gifts Foundation

Since 1982

The Gift Clarification Process was developed by the Gathering Gifts Foundation, an organization dedicated to helping individuals, families, and teams discover their unique contributions. For over four decades, this work has helped thousands of people name what has always been true about them — and live more fully from that place of clarity.
