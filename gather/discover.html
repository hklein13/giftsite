Discover Your Gift

Here are four questions to answer to help Discover your Gift:

01

Recall a time you helped another person or contributed to a project. What is it you did? What qualities or attributes did you bring to that endeavor?

02

Think about someone you admire. What are three qualities or attributes you truly appreciate about that person?

03

Identify three qualities that you appreciate about yourself.

04

If we were to invite three people into this conversation who have seen the "best of you" in various situations throughout your life what are the first names of the three people we would interview?

What would each person say is your Unique Gift?
