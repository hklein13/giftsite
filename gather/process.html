The Gift Clarification Process

How Discovery Unfolds

This isn't a personality test or a skills assessment. It's a process of reflection, dialogue, and mutual recognition. The most meaningful insights emerge not from algorithms, but from conversations with people who see you clearly.

01

Reflect

Begin by looking inward. Consider the moments when you've felt most alive, most useful, most yourself. What were you doing? Who were you with? What felt effortless?

02

Dialogue

Share your reflections with people who know you well. Listen as they affirm and refine what you've sensed about yourself. This is where the real discovery happens — face to face.

03

Clarify

Name your Gift. Not as a label or a limitation, but as a compass — something you can return to when making decisions, building relationships, or finding your path forward.

"The real power of this work lives inside of dialogue — hearing affirming reflections from others, face to face or over the phone."

The heart of the Gift Process

Who is this for?

The Gift Process works for individuals, families, and teams who want to discover and appreciate each other's unique contributions.
