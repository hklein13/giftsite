Every person carries within them a unique Gift.

Yet many of us move through life without ever naming it.

We sense it in moments of flow, in the gratitude of others, in the quiet satisfaction of work that feels meaningful.

But we haven't found the words.

The Gift Site exists to help you discover and articulate what has always been true about you.
